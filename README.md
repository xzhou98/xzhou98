## 🔬 Research Interests
> **Exploring Vulnerabilities in Frontier LLMs and LRMs**  
> I study jailbreaks, prompt-based attacks, and adversarial in-context learning in frontier large language models and large reasoning models. I also analyze the mechanisms behind these failures to better understand how unsafe or misleading context can alter model behavior and reasoning.
>
> **Robust and Effective Safety Alignment for LLMs & LRMs**  
> I work on fine-tuning methods for improving the safety and helpfulness of large language models and large reasoning models. Using supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement learning, I aim to build models that remain aligned even under adversarial or challenging conditions.
>
> **LLM Unlearning**  
> I develop methods for removing unwanted or sensitive knowledge from language models while preserving useful capabilities. My goal is to enable precise and effective unlearning without causing unnecessary damage to general model performance.
>
